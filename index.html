Yes, CarHub is currently offering Car Inspection at Home for a hassle-free selling experience. To check if the Home Inspection service is available at your location, please select your city while booking the appointment.


Can I reschedule my appointment?

What precautions should I take to ensure the warranty remains valid?

For the warranty to continue being valid, the buyer must ensure that the car needs to be serviced by you typically every 6 months or 10,000 km (whichever occurs first)
